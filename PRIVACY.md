# Privacy Policy for R97 AI

**Last Updated: May 22, 2026**

At R97 AI, we prioritize the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle, process, and protect your data when you interact with our platform.

---

## 1. Data Collection & Processing

R97 AI is built on a "Privacy-First" architecture. We do not store your personal conversations on our servers permanently unless explicitly requested for feature improvements.

### A. Conversation Data
- **Chat Logs**: When you send messages, they are processed through **NVIDIA's Meta Llama 3.1 NIM** endpoints. These requests are transient and used solely to generate a response.
- **Voice Data**: Real-time voice calls use the **Gemini 1.5 Flash Live API**. Audio streams are processed in real-time to facilitate emotional resonance and vocal responses.

### B. Third-Party API Integration
We utilize industry-leading AI providers. Your data is subject to their respective privacy terms:
- **NVIDIA**: Data sent to `integrate.api.nvidia.com` is governed by NVIDIA's Enterprise Privacy Policy.
- **Google Gemini**: Voice interactions and multimodal processing are governed by Google's Generative AI Data Processing Terms.

---

## 2. Information Security

### A. Data in Transit
All communications between your browser and our servers, and between our servers and AI providers, are encrypted using **TLS 1.3 (SSL)**.

### B. No Third-Party Selling
We **never** sell, rent, or trade your personal information or conversation history to third-party marketers or data brokers.

---

## 3. User Controls

### A. Session Persistence
By default, conversation history is stored in your browser's **Local Storage**. You can clear this at any time by using the "Clear Chat" feature within the application or clearing your browser cache.

### B. Voice Privacy
In voice mode, R97 AI is designed to ignore reading out sensitive technical strings such as URLs, long code blocks, or system logs to ensure your audio environment remains clean and professional.

---

## 4. Children's Privacy
R97 AI is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children.
